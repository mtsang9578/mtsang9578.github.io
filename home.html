WHAT WE DO

Community Friendship, Inc. (CFI) is a non-profit organization that has been accredited by the International Commission on Accreditation of Rehabilitation Facilities (CARF), for more than 37 years.This accreditation indicates that CFI respects the rights and individuality of those we serve, and that we have made a direct commitment to put the needs of our consumers at the center of everything we do. We are a comprehensive provider of recovery based services assisting adults in Metropolitan Atlanta. Our mission is to provide a supportive community for people whose mental illnesses prevent them from participating in community life.

Program Services

Psychosocial Rehabilitation Services

Provides consumers with the opportunity to develop and practice vocational, social and living skills.

Housing Services

We offer a wide array of supported housing programs for individuals in the community including group homes, supervised apartments, and independent apartments.

Work Opportunities Program

We help consumers choose, get and keep competitive jobs in the community.

Peer Support Services

We provide a consumer managed program, operated by Certified Peer Specialists to assist in developing and maintaining recovery, wellness, and daily living skills.
